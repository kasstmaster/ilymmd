Follow the steps below to watch with Plex and request new titles through Jellyseerr.

Android TV / Google TV / Fire TV

  • Open the Play Store (or Amazon Appstore on Fire TV) and install Plex.
  • Open Plex and sign in with your Plex account.
  • Go to “Home” and you should see our library after you’re shared in.
    • Troubleshooting tips
    • Can't see the library? Sign out/in again, then open “Sources” and check that our server is selected.
    • Clock mismatch: Make sure your device’s date/time is correct (secure connections need accurate time).
    • Network limits: Some guest/hotel Wi-Fi blocks streaming. Try another network or your home Wi-Fi.

    Roku (Plex)

    You have two ways to watch Plex on Roku:

    Option 1 – Sign in on Roku

  • From the Channel Store, install Plex.
  • Open Plex, choose Sign In, and follow the code pairing on your phone or at https://app.plex.tv
  • Once signed in, our shared library will appear automatically.

    • Option 2 – Cast from your phone

  • Install the Plex app on your Roku from the Channel Store (you don’t need to sign in on Roku).
  • On your phone, open the Plex app and connect to our server.
  • Tap the Cast button (TV icon with Wi-Fi waves) and select your Roku device.
  • The movie or show will start playing on your Roku TV.

    • Note: Casting requires your phone and Roku to be on the same Wi-Fi. Playback depends on your phone staying connected, and you may see fewer features compared to signing in directly on Roku.

    Troubleshooting tips
    • No library? If using Option 1, make sure you’re signed in with the correct Plex account that has been shared to.
    • Cast button missing? Ensure your Roku and your phone are on the same Wi-Fi network, and both apps are updated.
    • Update apps: On Roku: Settings → System → System update → Check now, then reopen Plex.

    Requesting New Titles

    Use the Discover section in the app via your phone, tablet, or computer. Search for a title and tap Request. Requests do not work from TV apps.
