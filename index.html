Android TV / Google TV (most Smart TVs) & Fire TV

  • Open the Play Store (or Amazon Appstore on Fire TV) and install Jellyfin.
  • Open Jellyfin → choose Add Server (or Manually add).
  • Enter the server URL: https://ilymmd.tail9854e5.ts.net
  • Select Next → log in with your account (we'll provide it if needed).
  • You're in! Browse your libraries and start watching.
    • Troubleshooting tips
    • Cannot connect? Double‑check the URL has https:// and no typos.
    • Clock mismatch: Make sure your TV's date/time is correct (TLS needs accurate time).
    • Network limits: Hotel/guest Wi‑Fi can block streaming ports. Try another network or your home Wi‑Fi.
    • Still stuck? Restart the TV and the Jellyfin app, then try again.

    Roku

  • On your Roku, open the Channel Store and install Jellyfin.
  • Open Jellyfin → choose Sign in → Enter server manually.
  • Type the server: https://ilymmd.tail9854e5.ts.net and continue.
  • Log in with your account to finish setup.
    • Troubleshooting tips
    • Connection failed? Re‑enter the URL carefully; Roku's on‑screen keyboard loves to drop the s in https.
    • Update apps: Settings → System → System update → Check now, then retry Jellyfin.

    Requesting New Titles

    Use Discover on your phone, tablet, or computer. Search for a title and tap Request. Requests do not work from TV apps.
